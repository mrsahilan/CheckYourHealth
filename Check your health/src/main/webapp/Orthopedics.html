Orthopedics Doctors

The Department of Orthopedic Surgery at RCSH provides expert medical and surgical treatment for a spectrum of conditions, from minor injuries to complex trauma, including hip, knee, spine, hand, or bone cancer surgeries. Our team consists of well-qualified orthopedic surgeons trained in various specialties at renowned institutes, along with therapists focused on providing individualized, passionate care. The teams started in 2015 have been growing with excellent services to polytrauma patients, keyhole surgeries, sports medicine, and joint replacement.
